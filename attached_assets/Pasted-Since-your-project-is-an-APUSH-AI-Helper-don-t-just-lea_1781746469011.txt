Since your project is an **APUSH AI Helper**, don't just leave it as a basic chatbot. Turn it into something that actually solves a problem for APUSH students.

Here are upgrades you can add in Replit:

### 1. APUSH Study Modes

Instead of one chat box, add:

* 📚 Topic Explainer

  * Explains any APUSH topic
  * Example: "Explain the Market Revolution"

* 📝 SAQ Generator

  * Creates APUSH-style Short Answer Questions

* 📖 DBQ Helper

  * Helps brainstorm DBQ documents and outside evidence

* 🎯 Multiple Choice Practice

  * Generates APUSH MCQs with explanations

* ⚡ LEQ Planner

  * Creates thesis statements and essay outlines
